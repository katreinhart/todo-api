About Kat
Kat has been programming in some form or another for almost 25 years. Starting with BASIC at age 6 and continuing with C++ in high school, then on to web-based technologies, she's always found coding to be fun and challenging. Though never formally employed in a development setting, she is familiar with a wide range of web technologies including HTML, CSS, JS and jQuery, as well as front-end design technology including Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, etc. She is trying to learn (and demonstrate) enough to land a junior full-stack development position at a company with a solid diversity policy working on amazing and ethical projects.
